Science Exhibition

Sanskar Gyanpeeth images Science Exhibition
site_admin 0 Comments

“Science begins with wonder, scientific temperament and rational thinking”Portraying a year-long interesting and relentless journey of learning, exploring and implementing, the multifarious students of SANSKAR GYANPEETH 10+2 School showcased their exemplary skills in Science Exhibition. The students demonstrated live experiments of the innate learning gathered from Various topics. The proud parents were spellbound by the mesmerizing skills of their children and the day remained etched in their memories forever. The whole exhibition was blessed by eminent personalties Chief Guest Dr.Ashok kumar(Head of HR and School system at University Centres of Leeds Trinity United Kingdom,Guest of honour Mr.Sunil Kr Prajapati(BDO Baghmara) Mr.Kamal Kishore Singh (CO Baghmara),Mr.Neeraj Kumar (officer incharge Barora P.S) Chittaranjan Kumar (GM block 2 area)and our Parents. The assiduous students showcased their exemplary learning and practical skills of science during the highly successful exhibition held Yesterday 21th February’ 2023 at our school campus…..The school is committed to the pursuit of academic excellence and all round development of each and every child. SANSKAR ,GYANPEETH(SGP) ,HARINA, ,Baghmara, Dhanbad provides ample opportunities to our children to grow and progress in a modern and scientific way.
